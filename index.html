Our Story

TEI-HA CONSTRUCTION SERVICES LTD is a Uganda private construction company/Architectural Consulting firm acknowledged in technology based services for built and natural environment in form of Project Planning,Designing,Surveying,Management,Quantifying and Project delivery services.

In that regard, Tei-ha Construction Services Ltd is committed to the provision of innovative, sustainable value for money, financially affordable, economically viable, socially acceptable and environmentally friendly engineering construction/consultancy services and continual improvement tailored to customer satisfaction

TEI-HA construction Services Limited, is a multidisciplinary Engineering firm with its Headquarters in Nansana -Wakiso, Uganda. The film, through a team of professionally qualified and widely experienced personal, supplies technologybased intellectual services in the form of project planning, designing. Surveying, quantifying and management of project delivery in the built environment industry.

Featured Projects

A glimpse into our world of architectural innovation.

BULINDO PROJECT

A blend of modern design and sustainable practices.

The project was initially contracted to a different firm, which failed to convince the client about the project safety, durability and stability.

Consequently, Tei-ha Construction Services Ltd. was consulted to handle all site issues thereby, thinking both creatively and logically to solve drawings and difficulties they faced due to improper geotechnical test which was previously done. We encountered challenges with sloping topography that was misinterpreted on foundation and basement level and mica soils with larger spans in all directions, as well as a 2.8m cantilever slab. This necessitated a comprehensive assessment by Qualified tested Engineer, giving us an opportunity to re- Design the project based on site condition as Design and build project.

RESIDENTIAL PROJECT at Kyabakunza-Masaka city

Innovative smart home features for enhanced living.

Because of the need to provide complete solutions to the changing dynamics in the construction industry, TEI-HA Construction Services Ltd has undertaken a number of design stage up to and including implementation of the construction.

This is especially in complicated designs or where the implementation period is limited by time constrains.

MATERIAL SUPPLY PROJECT

Maximizing natural light and energy efficiency.

Tei-ha participated fully in supply of construction materials for upgrade of UNHCR office premises and staff accommodation in Kiryandongo refugee settlement and civil construction in Lamwo Refugee settlement under the contract agreement between Tei-ha Construction Services Ltd and Latent Company Ltd.
